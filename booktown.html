Overview

What is BookTown?

BookTown is an Android application developed to help students and book lovers buy, sell, and resell both new and used books. The platform is optimized for local publication books and simplifies the college book exchange process through smart categorization, live tracking, and recommendations.

User Roles

  • Admin: Manages books, users, and system settings.
  • Buyer: Browses and purchases books.
  • Seller: Uploads and manages their own books for sale.
  • Delivery Person: Assigned to orders for delivery and OTP verification.

Key Features

  • Secure login with role-based authentication (Firebase)
  • Book upload with photo, description, and price
  • Search by name, author, or ISBN
  • Admin verification of books & sellers
  • Order placement with invoice & payment options (COD/online)
  • Delivery assignment with OTP confirmation
  • Resell feature only available after subscription approval
  • Personalized book recommendations based on purchase history
